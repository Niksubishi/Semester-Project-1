ABOUT US

Step into the vibrant world of discovery at our Community Science Museum, where the wonders of science await you! From the depths of the ocean to the far reaches of space, our museum is a celebration of humanity's ongoing quest for knowledge and understanding. This has been our goal since we first opened our doors in 1909!

At our museum, we are on a mission to make science accessible to all, fostering a space where curiosity thrives and discoveries are celebrated. Our goal is to spark a lifelong passion for exploration and learning, igniting the flame of curiosity in every visitor.

Gone are the days of dry textbooks and dull lectures. Here, science bursts to life through over a thousand captivating exhibits, each one inviting you to interact, explore, and marvel at the wonders of the natural world. Whether you're unleashing the power of electricity, uncovering the secrets of the cosmos, or delving into the mysteries of the microscopic world, there's something here to inspire and delight everyone.

Admission

The entrance is free for all. There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are £7.00 per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum. If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

Food and Drink

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

Shop

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you’ve left the museum.
